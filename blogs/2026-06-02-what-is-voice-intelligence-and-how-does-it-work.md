Voice intelligence uses AI to analyze spoken conversations and extract actionable insights beyond basic transcription, combining automatic speech recognition, natural language processing, sentiment analysis, and topic detection. With 65% of customers feeling most companies treat them like a number, voice intelligence transforms raw audio into structured, actionable data that helps businesses understand customer interactions at scale.
